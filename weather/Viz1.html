Temperature vs. Latitude

"Is there a trend in weather characteristics as we approach the equator?"

The temperature vs. latitude shows a strong relationship between the temperature and the cities distance from the equator. As this visualization shows a maximum temperature around the equator. Temperatures decrease as the distance from the equator increases in both positive and negative latitudes. However, there appears to be a greater decrease in temperature going into the Northern Hemisphere at this type of year. This may be a result of the distance from the sun in the Northern and Southern Hemispheres.
